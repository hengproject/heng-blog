这篇论文虽然没有提出实用的参数高效训练方法，但通过实验发现：神经网络虽然拥有大量参数，但完成任务所需的有效优化自由度可能远低于参数总量。这一观察为后来寻找结构化低维更新方法（如 LoRA）提供了理论动机。


论文主要论证了： 完成任务所需的有效优化自由度可能远低于参数总量
In a D-dimensional parameter space, optimization restricted to a much smaller d-dimensional random subspace can still achieve 90% of the full-training baseline performance. This suggests that, when d is sufficiently large, the random subspace has a great chance of intersecting the set of good solutions in the full parameter space, allowing optimization to reach a high-performing parameter configuration.

$\theta = \theta_0 + Pz$

$\operatorname{Col}(P)$
